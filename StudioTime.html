Mixing and Mastering

Our mixing and mastering service allows anyone to achieve that unique sound they’ve been looking for in their final mix. Our engineers are easy to talk to and well versed in multiple styles of music and sound design, ensuring an industry-ready product.

There is a 2 hour minimum purchase for $40 and every additional hour is $20.

Recording Breakdown

We offer studio recording time with one of our three audio engineers. We have all the resources needed to make the recording process as easy and fluent as can be, while still delivering on commercial quality sound.

Pricing is $15 per hour. If you book 4 hours it's $50 or you can get 8 hours for $95 with $15 per additional hour.

Cancellations and Refund Policy

Our company sticks to a very strict and simple two-point refund policy with all our services, with the exception of our Catalog Music option. All products are sold as-is when your session is completed. The standard hourly rate will apply to any extended time needed. If you are dissatisfied within the first sixty minutes of any session, a full refund will be given and the entire session will be deleted. If you are using our Fresh Rate service you be given one day to opt out and request a full refund.
