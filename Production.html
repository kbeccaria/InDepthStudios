Production Services

Style and Direction

Original Production at its Finest

Producing music can be a challenge, that's why we have two experienced producers and mixers to help you navigate the way to bringing your music to life.



Fresh Rate Service

The Fresh Rate service allows for you to have a free consultation with one of our engineers and discuss what style and direction you are looking for in the original song or production you would like us to create. We ask that you come prepared with a few sample or comparison tracks to help us better understand what sound you may be looking for. Our engineers are very flexible and intuitive when it comes to creating an original piece for our clients.


This service is offered at $50 flat rate for one week of services which includes 20 hours of producing time and an open line of communication during the entire process leading to the final product at the end of seven days.
Contact us for more info.



One-on-One Production

Our One-on-One option is a more hands-on and integrated method to approaching the production of your original piece. You will be working side-by-side with one of our engineers through the entire process, allowing for maximum input and communication to ensure exceptional results.


This service is offered at $20 per hour or discounted if you book 4 hours for only $65 and $135 for 8 hours.
Contact us for more info.



Cancellations and Refund Policy

Our company sticks to a very strict and simple two-point refund policy with all our services, with the exception of our Catalog Music option. All products are sold as-is when your session is completed. The standard hourly rate will apply to any extended time needed. If you are dissatisfied within the first sixty minutes of any session, a full refund will be given and the entire session will be deleted. If you are using our Fresh Rate service you be given one day to opt out and request a full refund.
