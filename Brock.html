Brock's Music Services

Skills on Skills

Bringing Your Notes to Life

Brock Stone is our lead guitar technician and fellow composer with over a decade of honing his guitar playing and music theory skills. He is eager to offer his personal insight in different ways to help you achieve your project goals.



Song Writing Services

Brock can write and compose an original song for you in two weeks. He is also able to write an original melody or rift you may be looking for, instead of an entire composed piece. Pricing is $50 for an entire piece, composed within 2 weeks or $25 for an original melody or rift which can be made available in a few days.
Contact us for more info.



Session Guitarist

For anyone who needs a guitarist to play and record an original piece that you may already have, Brock is your guy. His years of guitar playing allow him to adapt to any style piece you have, providing maximum authenticity to your work. Pricing is $35 per hour for the reproduction and recording of the project.
Contact us for more info.



Vocal Consultant

With years of music theory and ear training, Brock is available for in-session consultation while you are recording your piece. He's helping to ensure your best work for only $10 per hour.
Contact us for more info.



Cancellations and Refund Policy

Our company sticks to a very strict and simple two-point refund policy with all our services, with the exception of our Catalog Music option. All products are sold as-is when your session is completed. The standard hourly rate will apply to any extended time needed. If you are dissatisfied within the first sixty minutes of any session, a full refund will be given and the entire session will be deleted. If you are using our Fresh Rate service you be given one day to opt out and request a full refund.
